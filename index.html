3-Day Outlook: Tuesday -  Thursday

High pressure will build across the Valley today with partly sunny to mostly cloudy skies. A weak trough will bring a chance for some isolated showers this afternoon.. Mostly clear skies are on tap tonight with partly sunny skies developing Wednesday as another cold front approaches from the Great Lakes.

Chances for showers increase Wednesday afternoon with showers and isolated thunderstorms expected. Showers are likely Wednesday night into Thursday morning as the front moves across the area. Showers continue Thursday night as the upper-level trough associated with the surface cold front moves across the region through Friday afternoon.
