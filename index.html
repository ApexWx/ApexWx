Short-Term Outlook: Thursday -  Saturday

High pressure over the Southeast will channel warm, humid air into the Valley today through Friday. A cold front will approach and cross the SJV Friday into Saturday with showers and afternoon thunderstorms. High pressure will build into the Valley behind the front with drier, less humid air.
