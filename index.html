Short-Term Outlook: Sunday -  Tuesday

Mostly cloudy skies with areas of fog have overspread the region this morning as a marine layer of air has settled over the state. Skies are expected to become partly sunny this afternoon as surface high pressure centered to the northeast and an upper-ridge of high pressure remain across the SJV today. It will be a quiet day weather-wise with only a slight chance for isolated showers in Fort Kent and the SJV. Dew points will be in the 60s through the short-term period making the air feel rather muggy.

Partly cloudy skies are expected tonight with increasing chances for showers activity after midnight as Henri remains stalled over southern New England and weakens over the course of the day tomorrow. Current models indicate that the remnants of the system will get caught up in the prevailing westerlies and track eastward near coastal Maine overnight Monday into Tuesday.

For Fort Kent and surrounding locations, mostly cloudy skies with a chance for showers are anticipated Monday with showers continuing Monday night into Tuesday morning. Henri's remnants will move into the Maritimes early Tuesday with lingering moisture contributing to some scattered showers and afternoon thunderstorms across the SJV Tuesday. High pressure will build into the Valley Tuesday night with mostly clear skies developing.

Medium-Range Outlook: Wednesday - Saturday

High pressure builds in Wednesday with mostly sunny skies that become partly cloudy Wednesday night. A slight chance for isolated showers is expected Wednesday. Dew points will remain in the 60s Wednesday and Thursday producing humid conditions.

Chances for showers increase Wednesday night as a cold front approaches with partly cloudy to partly sunny skies Thursday along with showers and some scattered thunderstorms. A chance for showers and isolated thunderstorms continue Thursday night with decreasing and precipitation ending Friday morning behind the cold front.

High pressure will build down from the northwest Friday and Saturday with lower humidity levels, cooler temperatures and partly cloudy to mostly sunny skies across the SJV.
