3-Day Outlook: Wednesday - Friday

High pressure build across the SJV this Wednesday with mostly clear skies in the morning becoming increasingly cloudy in the afternoon as a warm front lifts towards the region and stalls out to the south later today. Partly cloudy skies and mostly dry conditions are expected overnight with only isolated precipitation expected.

An occluded front approaches Thursday with a chance for showers by the afternoon with rain showers likely overnight into Friday morning as the occlusion moves across the SJV. Partly sunny skies are expected Friday as the front moves east into the Maritimes.

A cold front trailing from low pressure near James Bay in Canada will bring a chance for evening rain showers followed by a rain/snow shower mix overnight into Saturday as the front moves closer to the region.
