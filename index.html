Three-Day Outlook: Friday - Sunday

An occluded front will approach and cross the Valley today bringing rain this afternoon and evening tapering to showers during the overnight hours. Partly sunny skies will develop Saturday as high pressure briefly builds across the region. Another area of low pressure will approach from the southwest and cross the SJV Saturday night with rain mixing with snow in the early morning hours Sunday. Mostly cloudy to partly cloudy skies are expected across the region Sunday.

Today & Tonight
Mostly cloudy this morning then overcast with a 100% chance for rain. Between 0.10 and 0.25-inch rainfall expected. High around 42° with southeast winds 13-16 mph gusting 30-35 mph this afternoon and evening.

Tonight, cloudy with a 100% chance for rain tapering to showers after midnight. Rainfall totals in the 0.25 to 0.50-inch range. Patchy fog possible overnight. Low near 36° with winds becoming south-southwest at 2-4 mph.

Saturday, November 13
Partly sunny with a 30% chance for showers. Less than 0.10-inch precipitation expected. High near 48° with west-southwest shifting to the southeast 2-3 mph.

Saturday night, mostly cloudy with a 70% chance for rain mixing with snow after midnight. Precipitation totals between 0.10 and 0.25-inch. Low around 33° with west winds 2-3 mph.

Sunday, November 14
Mostly cloudy in the morning then becoming partly cloudy in the afternoon with 30% chance for rain/snow mix, mainly in the morning. Precipitation totals less than 0.10-inch. High around 42°. West winds 5-10 mph.

Sunday night, partly cloudy with a 20% chance for precipitation. Low near 28°. Southwest winds 0-7 mph.

Days 4 to 7: Monday - Thursday

An area of low pressure tracking from the Mid-Atlantic coast into the Maritimes Monday into Tuesday may bring the first appreciable snowfall to the region. Snow showers continue Tuesday before tapering off in the afternoon. Partly sunny/partly cloudy skies are expected Wednesday with a chance for afternoon rain/snow showers as a warm front lifts across the Valley. A cold front moves through Thursday with rain and snow showers.
