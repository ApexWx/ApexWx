Three-Day Outlook: Monday - Wednesday

High pressure over the region today and tonight will move east Tuesday with increased cloudiness over the region. A warm air mass will remain in place through midweek with daytime highs running about 15 to 20 degrees above normal for this time of year (~54° in Fort Kent). A weak trough approaches Wednesday with a chance for afternoon showers continuing into Wednesday night.
