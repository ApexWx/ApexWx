Short-Term Outlook: Thursday - Saturday

An off-shore front with subtropical moisture will back towards New  England today with an area of low pressure running along it. A chance for rain is possible as the low pressure area tracks along eastern Maine tonight before moving in to the Maritimes tonight as the front dissipates to the east.

High pressure builds into the Valley Friday with mostly sunny skies across the region. Mostly clear skies give way to partly cloudy skies Friday night with increasing clouds Saturday along with chances for showers as a cold front drops down from the northwest. Showers and afternoon thunderstorms are possible as the front approaches and moves through with showers continuing into the overnight hours Saturday night.
