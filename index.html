3-Day Outlook: Thursday - Saturday

Hazardous Weather Outlook: Low pressure is expected to move across the region later New Year`s Day into Sunday. This system has the potential to bring snow or rain to the region. The exact track of this system is still uncertain, as is the precipitation types. Please monitor the latest forecasts for the upcoming weekend.

A weak cold front trailing from a low pressure to the north will move across the Valley today with a chance for snow showers through the afternoon. High pressure will build across the region Friday with partly sunny skies. Low pressure will approach from the southwest Friday night and cross the region Saturday into Sunday drawing a cold front across the SJV. Snow is expected Friday night into Saturday morning with rain mixing in Saturday afternoon into the overnight hours. Precipitation changes to all snow early Sunday as colder air moves into the region. However, forecast model uncertainty is still high, so the system's track and strength will affect precipitation types and totals.
