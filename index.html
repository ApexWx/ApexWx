3-Day Outlook: Sunday - Tuesday

High pressure in Quebec will build into the region today and become centered over Maine tonight. Frost is expected tonight in the valleys of northwest Maine thanks to clear skies and calm winds. The high will drift eastwards Monday and Tuesday. Mostly clear and dry conditions are expected for the period.

Today & Tonight
Sunny with a high around 64° with north winds 6-10 mph.

Tonight, clear with a low near 34° with northwest to west winds near calm. Areas of frost across the North Maine Woods with frost and valley fog possible across much of northern Maine.
