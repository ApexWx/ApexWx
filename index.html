3-Day Outlook: Sunday - Tuesday

Surface high pressure will build across the Valley today and tonight then move southeast of the region as the day progresses Monday.

A frontal system approaching from the west will bring increasing clouds with a chance for some light rain Monday evening. The warm front looks to cross the Fort Kent area early Tuesday with rain  likely as the front moves through. A chance for rain continues through Tuesday night as the cold front approaches from the west.

4 to 7 Day Outlook: Wednesday - Saturday

An upper-level low approaching from the west will help enhance the development of a low pressure area tracking into the Gulf of Maine. Rain is likely Wednesday afternoon through Thursday night as the Gulf low phases with the upper-low over the Maritimes. The upper-level system will stall out over the Maritimes and keep cool conditions with morning rain/snow and afternoon rain across the region through Saturday, with chances for precipitation decreasing Saturday afternoon as the low is slowly pushed further east.
