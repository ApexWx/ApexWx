Short-Term Outlook: Tuesday -  Thursday

A warm front will lift across the region today followed by a cold front later today into early Tuesday. The front will stall out along the Downeast coast Thursday with an area of low pressure tracking along the boundary Thursday into Friday as the associated upper-level trough approaches from the west.

Medium-Range Outlook: Friday - Monday

The remnants of TS Elsa will track across the southern portion of the Gulf of Maine Friday into Saturday.  Low pressure will track across the Valley from the west with rain likely Friday morning into the afternoon hours then taper to showers overnight Friday into Saturday morning. The upper-level trough associated with the low slowly shifts eastwards over the weekend with a chance for showers and a mix of clouds and sun currently anticipated Saturday with drier air moving in with partly cloudy skies Sunday into Monday.
