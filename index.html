Three-Day Outlook: Tuesday - Thursday

An upper-level trough will move across the region today and tonight with considerable cloudiness and a chance for flurries and snow showers this afternoon into the evening hours. High pressure builds in Wednesday sunny skies and clear conditions overnight into Thanksgiving. Partly cloudy skies overspread the Valley Wednesday afternoon as low pressure approaches from the Great Lakes heading into Friday.

Today and Tonight
Partly sunny with a high around 30°. 20% chance for flurries and snow showers this afternoon. Northwest winds 9-12 mph. Wind chill values in the mid-to-upper 10s.

Tonight, partly cloudy with a low around 20°. Northwest winds 10-11 mph. Wind chill values in the low 10s to upper single digits above zero.

Wednesday, November 24
Sunny with a high around 34°. Northwest winds 9-11 mph. Wind chill values in the mid-to-upper 10s.

Wednesday night, mostly clear with a low around 22°. Northwest winds at 6 mph.

Thursday, November 25 / Thanksgiving Day
Mostly sunny in the morning then becoming partly cloudy in the afternoon with a 10% chance for precipitation. High near 40° with northwest winds 3-5 mph.

Thursday night, becoming mostly cloudy with a 30% chance for precipitation. Low around 30°. Northwest winds 0-7 mph.

Days 4 to 7: Friday - Monday

Low pressure approaches from the west Friday and interacts with the Maritime low pressure producing a chance for snow showers Friday morning mixing with rain showers Friday afternoon. Precipitation changes to all snow that continues overnight into Saturday. Accumulating snow is expected to continue through Saturday afternoon before tapering to snow showers. Snow showers linger into Sunday as the upper-low drifts towards Labrador. Currently, Monday looks to be mostly dry with considerable cloudiness across the SJV.

Friday, November 26
Mostly cloudy to overcast with a 70% chance for morning snow showers mixing with rain showers and evening. Around 1-3 inches snowfall possible. High around 36° with northwest winds 0-7 mph.

Friday night, mostly cloudy with a 70% chance for rain/snow mix. Around 1-3 inches snowfall possible. Low near 26°. West winds 0-7 mph.

Saturday, November 27
Mostly cloudy with a 60% chance for snow tapering to snow showers in the afternoon. High near 33°. West winds 8-14 mph.

Saturday night, mostly cloudy with a 40% chance for snow showers. Low around 21°. West winds 8-14 mph.

Sunday, November 28
Mostly cloudy with a 20% chance for snow showers tapering off in the afternoon. High around 31°. West winds 8-14 mph.

Sunday night, mostly cloudy with a 10% chance for snow showers. Low near 18°. West winds 0-7 mph.

Monday, November 29
