Short-Term Outlook: Saturday - Monday

Low clouds will partially diminish this afternoon, before a weak cold front drifts down from the north this afternoon and tonight. This frontal boundary is expected to become positioned more north to south by Sunday as it slows or stalls out during the midday and afternoon hours. This weather feature will help to focus numerous showers and scattered thunderstorms along and ahead of it. Some of the storms could bring brief strong wind gusts and locally heavy rainfall.
