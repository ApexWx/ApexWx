Short-Term Outlook: Thursday - Saturday

Mostly sunny skies are on tap today with  winds diminishing in the afternoon. Clouds increase Thursday night as a clipper system moving over the Great Lakes draws an occluded front across the region with snow developing after midnight. Fawkes House can expected up to 1 inch snow accumulation from this fast-moving system. High pressure builds in with partly cloudy skies Friday afternoon through Sunday morning. A front brings some increased cloudiness Sunday with partly cloudy skies developing Sunday night.

*UTC / Z / GMT = Coordinated Universal Time / Zulu Time / Greenwich Mean Time -- Time is based on 0° longitude running through the Royal Observatory in Greenwich, London, UK.
