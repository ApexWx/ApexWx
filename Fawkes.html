Medium-Range Outlook: Thursday - Sunday

A more organized and stronger low pressure system will develop and bring activity along the cold front to the region by Thursday. Showers and thunderstorms are expected to develop along this cold front Thursday afternoon before it exits to the south Thursday night/Friday morning. The weekend, at this point, looks to be mainly dry as surface high pressure settles overhead.
