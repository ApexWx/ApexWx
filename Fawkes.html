Days 4 to 7: Thursday -  Sunday

Moist low-level southeast flow off of the Atlantic is expected Thursday through Saturday-- the result of an anomalous surface high parked north of New England and an upper
low over the Mississippi Valley. The best chance of more significant rain should hold off until Friday into Saturday, as low level jet and plume of deep moisture linked to remnants of upper low from the Lower Mississippi Valley lift northeast across Pennsylvania.
