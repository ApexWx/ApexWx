Three-Day Outlook: Wednesday - Friday

The Nor'easter off the Mid-Atlantic coast will move eastwards today with diminishing clouds and wind this afternoon and evening. High pressure will build across Pennsylvania tonight into Thursday. Clouds increase tomorrow afternoon as a low pressure system over the Mid-Mississippi Valley moves eastwards with rain overspreading the Lower Susquehanna Valley tomorrow night. Rain is likely Friday and Friday night as the low pressure area crosses the state.
