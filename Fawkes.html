Apex Wx Three-Day Outlook:  Friday - Sunday

High pressure will bring partly cloudy to partly sunny skies to the Lancaster region today. A weak upper-level disturbance will move southeast from the Upper Great Lakes bringing some light rain and snow showers to the area overnight. High pressure will bring partly cloudy to mostly clear skies Saturday and Saturday night followed by partly sunny skies Sunday with increasing clouds in the afternoon along with a chance for showers as a cold front approaches from the west. A chance for showers continues overnight into Monday.

Yesterday's high was 60° and the overnight low was 40°.

Lancaster's average high for December 3 is 48° and the average low is 30°.
