Medium-Range Outlook: Thursday - Sunday

The late week period may become more unsettled with an upper trough over the eastern US and a stationary front near the East Coast. The best chance of showers and storms will remain closest to where the stationary boundary sets up in PA's eastern and southeast counties. By the end of the week, the Bermuda high will slide onto the Atlantic coast funneling subtropical moisture up into the Lancaster area. Showers and storms driven by daytime heating will be possible across region with dew points and temperatures on the rise again. Locally heavy downpours may occur with any storms.
