The Week Ahead for Lancaster, Pennsylvania by Apex Wx
Updated: 27 February 2024 at 8:40 am EST

3-Day Outlook: Tue. Feb. 27 -  Thu.  Feb. 29

NWS State College Hazardous Weather Conditions
Click for the latest information.

Clouds will increase as a cold front approaches from the west today with showers likely this afternoon continuing through tomorrow evening before changing to snow overnight into early Thursday morning. Thunderstorms are also possible late tonight and over the day Wednesday, especially in the afternoon. Gusty south winds become westerly Thursday with decreasing clouds Wednesday night and mostly sunny skies and less breezy conditions Friday. Clear skies and cool temperatures are expected Thursday night into Friday.

4-7 Day Outlook: Fri. Mar. 1 - Mon. Mar. 4

Mostly sunny skies Friday morning become partly cloudy in the afternoon with the possibility of a coastal low bringing some rain Friday night through Saturday afternoon. Lingering showers taper off by early Sunday; however, isolated showers remain possible Sunday into Monday as another frontal system approaches from the west. Model uncertainty is rather high, so watch NWS updates for the latest information.
