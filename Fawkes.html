Apex Wx Lancaster Weather Summary

The Lancaster region will be on the periphery of a high pressure ridge offshore in the Atlantic. Return flow around the backside of the ridge will bring hot and humid conditions to the Lower Susquehanna Valley through the period with scattered strong to severe storms possible Monday and Tuesday afternoon. Daytime highs combined with humidity will lead to high heat indices Monday and Tuesday afternoons.
