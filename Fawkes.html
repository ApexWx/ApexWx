Short-Term Outlook: Sunday -  Tuesday

Hazardous Weather Outlook
Flood Watch in effect from August 22, 7:00 AM until August 23, 8:00 AM
An upper-level low will channel Tropical Cyclone Henri into Rhode Island / Connecticut today. Precipitation from the storm will wrap into the circulation around the upper-low with showers likely today and tonight across the Lancaster region with nearly 2-inches of rainfall possible. Embedded thunderstorms are also possible, especially this afternoon.

As Henri lifts to the northeast Monday, showers become more scattered in nature with a chance for afternoon thunderstorms. Another three-quarters an inch of rainfall is possible for Fawkes House and vicinity tonight and tomorrow for a storm total around 2.75-inches based on current model data.

A ridge of high pressure builds across the region Monday night with clearly skies and mostly sunny conditions Tuesday and clear skies Tuesday night allowing the region to dry out from the deluge.
