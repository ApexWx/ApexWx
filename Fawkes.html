Short-Term Outlook: Thursday - Saturday

High pressure will dominate Lancaster's weather today and tomorrow. A quasi-stationary front along the East Coast will see a low pressure area track north along the boundary. This system will bring a chance for mainly afternoon showers and thunderstorms to Fawkes House and vicinity Saturday with isolated showers/thunderstorms lingering into the overnight hours.

Medium-Range Outlook: Sunday - Wednesday

A trough will bring increased cloudiness and a chance for scattered afternoon showers and thunderstorms to Lancaster this Saturday. Isolated showers and thunderstorms are possible overnight into Sunday. High pressure over the Southeast will dominate the region's weather into midweek helping to pump in increasingly hot and humid weather for the second week in August. Maximum heat index values will surpass the 90F mark beginning on Sunday and will likely approach the 100F mark by midweek. Excessive heat advisories are possible Tuesday through Thursday.
