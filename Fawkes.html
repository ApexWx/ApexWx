Three-Day Outlook:  Monday - Wednesday

Skies will clear by this evening in the wake of a strong cold front that moved through the region. High pressure will build east into the region on gusty northwest winds which will subside this evening. Mostly sunny skies Tuesday with breezy northwest winds will remain mostly clear tomorrow night as high pressure continues to build into the area. Mostly sunny skies and less breezy conditions are expected Wednesday with partly cloudy skies developing Wednesday night.
