Medium-Range Outlook: Friday - Monday

A long-wave trough finally breaks down the ridge of high pressure late in the week with Friday perhaps a couple of degrees cooler. Models differ on the timing of the cold front at the end of the week with the European model depicting numerous showers and thunderstorms late in the day / early evening Fri day, while the slower American GFS model holds the cold front passage until the more stable Fri overnight hours, with less convection occurring.

Showers may linger across the south Saturday as the cold front slows down through Maryland and Virginia. Otherwise, the weekend looks pleasant - cooler and much less humid as high pressure builds in from the north and west.
