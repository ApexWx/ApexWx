NWS State College Hazardous Weather Outlook
Heat Advisory in effect from 11:00 AM EDT until 06:00 PM EDT today

Hot and humid conditions will prevail today with a heat advisory in effect from midday into the early evening hours. A weak disturbance brings a chance for scattered showers and thunderstorms to the Lancaster region this afternoon into the evening hours with scattered strong to severe storms possible (SPC threat level 2 of 5).

An upper-level trough will pivot eastward across the Great Lakes/Midwest on Wednesday while TS Elsa tracks from northern Florida through the Southeast towards the Delmarva Peninsula by early Friday morning. These features combined with ample low-level moisture
and instability to fuel rounds of showers and thunderstorms Wednesday through Thursday.
